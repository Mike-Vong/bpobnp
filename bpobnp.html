Section 04 · Proposal 3
Per-Interaction Incentive & Penalty
Reward or penalize each interaction based on adherence to 7 evaluated interaction behaviours. Threshold is meeting 5 of 7. Every interaction above or below threshold generates a micro-incentive or micro-penalty.
